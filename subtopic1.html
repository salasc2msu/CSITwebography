  • History of the Art Tablet
    • In this link, we take a look at the history of the art tablet and how it has evolved in recent times. The first digital drawing tablet was the RAND tablet, This was the first graphics tablet that allowed actual real time handwriting to be projected onto a screen while writing on the tablet.. It did so by connecting a display to a writing surface and receiving information to determine the position of the stylus. Since then, many tablets have come out until most recently the Wacom Cintiq Pro and the iPad Pro, which both boast power that has not been since in a drawing tablet before.
