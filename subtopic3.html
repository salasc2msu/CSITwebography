  • History of 3D Printing
    • One form of creating art that is groundbreaking and recent is 3D printing. The first 3D printer was made by Dr. Hideo Kodama in 1981(Chapman, 2023). This was done by combining a resin with UV lights. Although the earliest prototype created was in 1981, 3D printers did not become commercially available until 2006. The first of these was the RepRap. One of the fascinating things about the RepRap is that it was created with plastic parts that can be 3D printed. In other words, one can purchase a RepRap and create other 3D printers with the RepRap alone (Chapman, 2023). As the desire for 3D printing grows, the capabilities also grow as well, due to innovations that add quality of life improvements, making 3D printing more efficient and reliable.
